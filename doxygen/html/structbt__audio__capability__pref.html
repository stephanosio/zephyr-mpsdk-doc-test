uint8_t bt_audio_capability_pref::framing

Framing support value.

Unlike the other fields, this is not a preference but whether the capability supports unframed ISOAL PDUs.

Possible values: BT_AUDIO_CAPABILITY_UNFRAMED_SUPPORTED and BT_AUDIO_CAPABILITY_UNFRAMED_NOT_SUPPORTED.
