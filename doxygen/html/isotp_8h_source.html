95/*
96 * CAN ID filtering for ISO-TP fixed addressing according to SAE J1939
97 *
98 * Format of 29-bit CAN identifier:
99 * ------------------------------------------------------
100 * | 28 .. 26 | 25 | 24 | 23 .. 16 | 15 .. 8 | 7 .. 0 |
101 * ------------------------------------------------------
102 * | Priority | EDP | DP | N_TAtype | N_TA | N_SA |
103 * ------------------------------------------------------
104 */
